Recipes

  • Quinoa
    • Ingredients
      • 1 cup uncooked Quinoa
      • 1 teaspoon olive oil
      • 2 cups water
      • 2 teaspoons of salt
    • Instructions
      • Rinse uncooked quinoa
      • Add 2 cups water, 1 cup uncooked quinoa, 2 teaspoons of salt in cooker. Bring to boil.
      • Lower heat, cover the cooker and wait 15 minutes.
      • Turn off heat, and let it sit for 5 minutes covered.
  • Whole Wheat Bread
    • Ingredients
      • 4 cups whole wheat bread flour
      • 1 tablespoon of butter
      • 2 teaspoons of salt
      • 1 tablespoon of honey
      • 2 cups of water
      • 1 packet of yeast
    • Instructions
      • Warm 2 cups of water for a little above room temperature. Add packet of yeast and mix.
      • Add 1 tbsp of butter, 2 tsp of salt, 1 tbsp of honey and mix.
      • Add 2 cups of whole wheat bread flour and mix for 2-3 minutes. Keep adding and mixing until it stops sticking to the bowl.
      • Cover large bowl with oil or nonstick spray with loose plastic and leave for 20 minutes for it to grow.
      • Divide it into reasonable portions and put into metal loaf containers. Use oil/nonstick to cover beforehand. Cover the pans with lose plastic for 20 minutes.
      • Take off plastic and put containers into preheated stove at 450 C for 40 minutes.
  • Pasta
    • Ingredients
      • Uncooked Pasta
      • Oil
      • Water
      • Salt
    • Instructions
      • Add pasta, water, salt into cooker. Bring to boil.
      • Cover with lid that has holes. Wait until pasta looks soft. Do a taste test with spoon.
      • Once cooked, take the water out and put whatever else you want in there.
  • French Fries
    • Ingredients
      • Uncooked Potatoes
      • Oil
      • Salt/Pepper
    • Instructions
      • Add oil to cooker. Bring to high heat. Wait 7 minutes. Rinse then cut potatoes to resemble french fries.
      • Add cut pototes slowly to cooker. Wait until slight redding/browning on french fries and take them out of oil.
      • Repeat process until out of potatoes. Add condiments such as salt and pepper and mix together.
